Title: Math Painting
Description: An app that lets the user provide the start coordinates of geometric shapes such as squares,
rectangles, their dimensions and their colors and the program produces an image file canvas with the geometric
shapes drawn on it.

Objects:

  Square: x, y, side, color, draw(canvas)

  Rectangle: x, y, width, height, color, draw(canvas)

  Canvas: width, height, color, make(imagepath)
